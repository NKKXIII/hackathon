One of the most common problems we face in Kuwait is the high prices of residential real estate,
so everyone resorts to renting. The rent is draining the employees without returning to them with interest. So we created a new feature for the banks in which the customer enters the property data (land-house-apartment), so the bank buys the property. After verifying the accuracy of the data and fulfilling the conditions by the bank employee, the purchase process is completed, and the customer pays in installments so that he can own the property.
